The problem

Your next appointment may already be buried in your inbox.

Tattoo inquiries arrive through Instagram, email, website forms, Facebook, referrals, and text messages. Details are incomplete, responses take too long, and serious clients get mixed in with people who are only asking for prices.

Missed messages

New inquiries disappear across multiple channels.

Incomplete intake

Artists repeat the same questions about size, placement, style, and budget.

Slow quoting

Good leads lose interest while waiting for a response.

Unpaid holds

Appointments remain blocked while deposits still need to be chased.

How it works

From first message to confirmed appointment.

01

InkMgr brings website, email, and connected social inquiries into one place and creates a client record automatically.
